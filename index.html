About Me

Hi, My name is Moses Njuguna, born on June 5th. Here’s a little story about my journey...

My Background

I was born on 5th June a few years back and raised in Gatitu,Kandara - Murang'a County by my lovely parents.
It have really been a struggle since then but I thank God for keeping me alive upto now.
Growing up, I was raised in a Christian home, deeply rooted in the teachings and values of the Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA) Warutara.
Not to leave behind I was baptized in 2009 at PCEA Warutara, a significant moment in my life that solidified my faith and commitment to my spiritual journey. Growing up in a religious environment, this event further deepened my connection with my values and beliefs.
The Godliness foundation shaped my character and continues to guide many of my life choices today.
I joined Gatitu Primary School in the year 2009 at my village and i'm glad I completed my primary education in the year 2017 in the midst of other students.
I later Joined Njumbi High School the following year - 2018 and thats where I discovered my passion for technology.
After completing my secondary education in 2022 ,I felt unsure about which course to pursue and which institution to attend.
After careful consideration, I decided to join Zetech University in September 2022. Thanks to Esther Mwangi for helping out.
Since then, It’ve been on an exciting journey to explore the world of tech and development with the help of my friends and my lecturers.
Um Looking back, I am proud to be an alumni and grateful for the experiences and knowledge I gained during my time there
Truly God has good plans for me 🥺🤍

"But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength. They will soar on wings like eagles; they will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint." - Isaiah 40:31

My Tech Journey

I discovered my passion for technology in high school. After i Completed my high School, i joined Zetech University in September 2022.
I created my first website at the age of 18, which sparked my love for coding and innovation.
I have Created several projets including an e-commerce Website , Crime prediction System etc.

Via Curiosity I Joined e-Mobilis Technology Institute where i'm constantly expanding my skills in web development, Artificial Intelligence and software engineering. I enjoy building real-world solutions through code.

My love for problem-solving and creativity pushes me to build meaningful projects and I’m always looking for new opportunities to grow.Thats Moses
